ABOUT MMW

Makers Modular Workstations (MMW) was created to solve a simple problem: modern makers need flexible, modular, durable workstations that adapt to their tools, their space, and their workflow — not the other way around.

MMW designs and builds modular workstation systems that can grow, change, and evolve with you. Whether you’re a hobbyist, a professional fabricator, a 3D‑printing enthusiast, or a small workshop owner, our goal is to give you a workstation that works as hard as you do.

Our modular approach means every component — from the base frames to the tool‑specific modules — is engineered to be interchangeable, upgradable, and future‑proof. No more buying an entirely new bench every time your tools or projects change.

MMW PROMISE

Every MMW design will be modular, practical, and engineered to improve the maker’s workflow — whether it’s drilling, printing, sanding, assembling, or prototyping.
